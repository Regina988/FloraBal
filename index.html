FLORABAL

WHAT IS FLORABAL?

FloraBal is a rapid vaginal health diagnostic kit designed to detect infections quickly, easily, and without the need for a laboratory or clinical visit. It belongs to a new generation of FemTech solutions with the objective of making women's healthcare more accessible, private and user-friendly.

Using advanced biomarker detection technology, FloraBal delivers reliable results in minutes, helping users make informed decisions about their health.

How it works Who we are

THE PROBLEM

Vaginal infections affect millions of women in Mexico each year, yet the default response is self-medication, not diagnosis.

Delayed care

Women wait too long or never seek a proper diagnosis.

Self-medication

Symptoms treated without identifying the actual cause.

Wrong treatment

Misdiagnosis leads to recurring infections and complications.

This cycle drives up complications, including elevated STI risk and pregnancy issues while adding costs that could be avoided with a timely, accurate diagnosis.

OUR SOLUTION

15 min RESULTS
No lab NEEDED
Self-use AT HOME

FloraBal uses a lateral flow immunoassay, the same technology behind pregnancy tests, to detect biomarkers like sialidase, an enzyme linked to bacterial vaginosis.

Designed for self-sampling, it delivers accurate results in ~15 minutes with no laboratory processing required. Biochemical precision, built for everyday use.
